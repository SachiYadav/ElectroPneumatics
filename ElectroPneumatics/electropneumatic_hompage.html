    • Solenoid

    When electric current is passed through any coil, it generates magnetic field. That magnetic field causes a iron block to move if found within it's range. On this priciple solenoids work. When electrcic current is passed through it's coil, the iron bar connected to valve is moved causing opening and closing of valve. This solenoids are mounted on the valves and connected to electrical output signal.

    Reed Switches

    Reed switches work on principle of magnet. Pneumatic cylinders have magnets installed in their piston heads. Reed switches are mounted on the cylinder surface at desired place. When cylinder reaches to vicinity to reed switch installed, the reed switch is actuated. Depending on reed switch construction the output signal is obtained. If the normal position is normally open, then afetr actuation it closes and vice versa.
